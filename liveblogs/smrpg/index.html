Ocampo's Moon/Quesada's Swan Plays Super Mario RPG

Super Mario RPG is a video game developed as a joint production between Square and Nintendo, and released on the Super Famicom in Japan on March 9, 1996. The game is later shipped to North America under the name Super Mario RPG: Legend of the Seven Stars on May 13, 1996 for the Super Nintendo, later overshadowed by the release of the Nintendo 64 console, and its flagship title Super Mario 64 on September of that same year.
